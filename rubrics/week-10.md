# Rubric Week 10 - RSC Boundaries

## Scoring Model

All dimensions are scored from 0 to 5.

- 0: Missing
- 1: Severely insufficient
- 2: Partially working with major gaps
- 3: Meets minimum expectation
- 4: Solid implementation with minor gaps
- 5: Production-quality, clear tradeoff reasoning

| completeness | 20 |

| delivery_readiness | 15 |

## Hard Gate (must pass)

- Server/Client 邊界有明確證據與理由
- Must submit Cline transcript summary and human review notes.
- Must include at least one self-identified risk and mitigation.

## Pass Threshold

- Overall weighted score: **80/100**
- Any hard gate failure: **automatic fail**

## Reviewer Evidence Checklist

- [ ] Evidence for each scored dimension is attached.
- [ ] High-risk gaps are explicitly documented.
- [ ] Feedback includes specific next actions.

1. What is working well?
2. What risks remain?
3. What should be fixed first?
4. What should be practiced next week?
